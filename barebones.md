# Barebones App Functionality

1. **Barcode Scanning**: Scan product > Auto-decrease stock OR Open "Add Batch" form if new.
2. **Inventory Table**: List of medicines with Name, Stock, Cost Price, Selling Price, Expiry, Location.
3. **Low Stock Alerts**: Auto-highlight items below threshold (e.g., < 10 units).
4. **Expiry Tracking**: Visual warning for items expiring within 30 days.
5. **Profit Calculation**: Real-time stats on (Selling Price - Cost Price) * Sales.
6. **Batch Management**: Track multiple batches of the same medicine (diff expiry/price).
7. **Vendor System**: Link medicines to suppliers for one-click reordering lists.
